∞Timely

Posted at May 20, 2017 by Piotr Kuczynski tags: sponsorship

RVM receives sponsored account at Timely

After receiving financial grant from Toptal, we are obliged to record our time spent on RVM. When seeking for the best time tracking software, we stumbled upon Timely. It felt like the most convenient and good looking application, which perfectly suited our needs. Unfortunately, their free plan did not cover teams.

We reached out to Timely and we are happy to announce that they agreed to sponsor us with a free lifetime account for RVM!

Thank you so much, Marius!
